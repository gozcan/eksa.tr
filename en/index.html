— 02 / Philosophy
EKSA, since 2008

EKSA is designed for those who seek a life beyond the ordinary. Every project begins with the wind of the land, the salt of the sea, the light of the morning.

Every building we create is a collector's piece in its own right. Produced in limited numbers, built with materials that can accompany the passage of time, and offering its residents far more than what they possess.

Our architectural team works in partnership with five independent studios based in Europe and Istanbul. In every project we listen to the voice of the place; then we speak.
