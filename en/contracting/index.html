General Contracting & Turnkey Construction.

Istanbul · Since 2008

EKSA, operating within Ekşioğlu Group, is a general contracting firm based in Istanbul. Since 2008 we deliver turnkey general contracting for luxury residential, mixed-use, boutique hotel and urban renewal projects — together with independent architectural studios, under single-point accountability.

1. The General Contracting Service

EKSA's general contracting covers all operational and technical processes of a construction project, from start to finish. The scope includes pre-feasibility after land acquisition, architectural concept coordination, permit processes, excavation and groundworks, structural construction, finishes, MEP installation, façade, landscaping, final handover and warranty-period support.

2. Service Scope

2.1 Design and Permit Coordination

Coordination from concept design to executed projects with independent architectural studios in Europe, Istanbul and Bodrum. Municipal permit processes, environmental approvals and related institutional approvals.

2.2 Structural and Finishing Construction

Steel or reinforced-concrete load-bearing system to the latest seismic code. High insulation values (A+ energy class target), smart-building infrastructure, façade combinations of natural stone, wood and glass.

2.3 MEP Installation

VRF climate control, underfloor heating, smart-home systems, security infrastructure, generator and UPS systems. Long-life material selection engineered to minimise annual maintenance cost.

2.4 Landscaping and Amenities

Garden, open-area and amenity layouts designed with landscape architects. Long-lasting, low-water, climate-adapted plant selection.

2.5 Handover and Warranty

2-year structural and 5-year structural-frame warranty after handover. Annual maintenance programme, management plan and dues-structure support.

3. Project Types

EKSA contracting is offered for the following project types:

  • Luxury residential — limited-edition villa or apartment projects (Bebek, Yeniköy, Tarabya, Sarıyer, Ataşehir, Çamlıca)
  • Urban renewal — risky-building renewal under Law No. 6306 (details on the urban renewal page)
  • Boutique hotel — concept-driven hotels
  • Mixed-use — residential + retail + office combined projects

5. FAQ

What project sizes does EKSA contract?

Typically projects between 5,000 m² and 60,000 m² of built area. Smaller boutique projects and larger mixed-use projects may be discussed separately.

Is contract pricing fixed?

Most of our projects use a fixed lump-sum contract. Price and schedule are guaranteed to the owner; contractor risks remain with EKSA.

How long is the turnkey schedule?

18–30 months on average, depending on project scale. Detailed schedule is provided before contract.

Is EKSA also a real-estate developer?

Yes. EKSA offers both in-house developed projects and pure contracting services for third-party owners and investors.
