Cookie Policy.

Effective · 24 May 2026

This Cookie Policy describes the cookies used on the www.eksa.tr website operated by EKSA Yapı A.Ş., their purposes and how you can manage them.

1. What Is a Cookie?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device through your browser by the websites you visit. They are used to remember your interactions with the site, store preferences and measure performance.

2. Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

Required for the core functionality of the website. The site may not work as expected without them. These cookies cannot be disabled.

2.2 Performance Cookies

Vercel Speed Insights cookies are used to measure core web vitals such as page load speed (LCP, INP, CLS). The data collected is anonymised.

2.3 Analytics Cookies

Vercel Web Analytics cookies collect aggregate statistics such as visitor counts, page views and traffic sources. The data is not personally identifying; individual users are not tracked.

2.4 Third-Party Cookies

Cookies from third-party providers such as Google Fonts (for typefaces), Unsplash (for imagery) and Cloudflare CDN (for scripts) may be placed in your browser. Their own privacy policies apply.

3. Managing Cookies

Most browsers accept cookies by default. You can use your browser settings to delete cookies, block them, or be warned when a cookie is placed. The links below go to help pages for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Blocking strictly necessary cookies may cause parts of the site to malfunction.

4. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. The current version is always published on this page. For material changes, a prominent notice will be displayed on our website.

5. Contact

For questions about our cookie policy, please contact us at kvkk@eksa.tr.
